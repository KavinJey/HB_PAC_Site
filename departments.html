Khushpal Pawar, President and Balpreet Mangat, Vice President have been managing HB-PAC since 2014 with hopes of enriching student life at HB along with supporting HB's arts.

Roby G. is the Bhangra Team captain for the 2016-2017 school year. He has had formal training in Bhangra for 6 years and is proud to be representing HB-PAC.

Manrose Sekhon and Jasleen Dhaliwal are this years Giddha Coordinators. They both have 12 amount of years of Giddha experience split between them and through their teamwork and leadership they both will lead this years Giddha team.

Lisa A, Spoken Word Coordinator, has been writing poems for most of her life and now at HB-PAC she helps others write as well as perform their poems to varying audiences.

Simrat Bains, Production Coordinator, Simrat has several years of experience and a passion for shooting as well as directing movies and short films. Through his experience he helps the HB-PAC and Production Team organize and handle videos.

Rupreet Kaur, Dance Coordinator, through gained experience leads the 2016-2017 Dance Team. Rupreet has been dancing for her entire life and brings a lot of hip-hop, cultural, and folk dancing experience to the table.
